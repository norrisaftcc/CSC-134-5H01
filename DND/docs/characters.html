Character Creation

In DND Text Adventure, your journey begins with character creation. You'll choose a class that defines your character's abilities and playstyle, then roll for your starting attributes. As you progress through the game, you'll gain experience, level up, and earn stat points to further customize your character.

Character Attributes

Every character has six primary attributes that determine their capabilities:

Attribute Description Effects
Strength Physical power and combat ability Improves physical damage and reduces enemy combat effectiveness
Dexterity Agility, reflexes, and coordination Influences combat outcomes and stealth capabilities
Constitution Health, stamina, and endurance Affects overall health and physical resilience
Intelligence Knowledge, memory, and reasoning Important for magic-users and problem-solving challenges
Wisdom Perception, intuition, and willpower Helps with decision-making and magical resistance
Charisma Personality, leadership, and persuasiveness Influences social interactions (referred to as "Rizz" in the game)

Leveling and Stat Points

As you gain experience points (XP) from completing challenges and defeating enemies, your character will level up. Each level provides:

  • Increased overall effectiveness
  • One stat point to assign to any attribute
  • Periodic increases to your proficiency bonus
